About A's Cafe

A place to slow down
and savour the moment.

A's Cafe is a beloved local spot in Invercargill — known for expertly brewed coffee, fresh cabinet food, and a genuinely warm atmosphere that keeps people coming back day after day.

Whether you're stopping in for your morning flat white, catching up with a friend over lunch, or just looking for a quiet corner to recharge — A's Cafe is your place. We take pride in every cup we pour and every plate we serve.

What We Offer

Made fresh, every day.

From our first coffee of the morning to the last slice of cake in the afternoon — everything at A's Cafe is made with quality ingredients and a genuine love of food.

☕
Specialty Coffee

Expertly pulled espresso, velvety flat whites, smooth long blacks, and everything in between. We take our coffee seriously — and it shows in every sip.

🥐
Cabinet Food

Fresh-made cabinet food daily — savoury slices, filled rolls, salads, and sweet treats. Perfect to pair with your coffee or grab on the go.

🍳
Light Meals

Satisfying cafe classics made with fresh, quality ingredients. Great for a relaxed lunch or a mid-morning catch-up with something a little more substantial.

🧁
Sweet Treats

House-baked cakes, slices, and muffins that change regularly. The perfect excuse to pop in and see what's on the cabinet today.

🥤
Cold Drinks

Iced coffee, cold brew, fresh juices, and milkshakes. Cool down with something refreshing — whatever the weather in Invercargill!

🤎
Loyal Locals

We know our regulars by name and by order. A's Cafe is the kind of place where you're never just a customer — you're part of the family.
